Welcome to our website!

This is a sample page that includes the tracking script at the bottom of the HTML. The script will automatically collect visitor information and send it to your tracking server.

How it works

The tracking script collects the following information:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Screen resolution
  • Referrer (where the visitor came from)
  • Time of visit

All this data is sent to your tracking server and can be viewed in the admin dashboard.
